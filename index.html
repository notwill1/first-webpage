Scuba diving is the activity I am most passionate about. Exploring places that many others haven't been able to is one of the many things I enjoy about the sport. Watching the native wildlife in their natural habitat is another reason to enjoy the sub-surface life.

Aerial Photography

I enjoy using my DJI for pictures and videos of views I would never have been able to capture without it. The places you can explore and the sights you can see are almost limitless with the reach of a modern 'drone'.
